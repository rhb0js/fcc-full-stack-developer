Homemade Pizza

My pizza recipe

Ingredients

  • flour
  • water
  • cheese
  • tomato

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour with water to get a soft dough.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil to the yeast mixture and knead into a soft dough.
  3. Preheat the oven to 220-240°C (470°F) and heat a baking tray.
  4. Roll out the dough, spread the sauce, and add cheese and toppings.
